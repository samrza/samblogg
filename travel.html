Travel

Places I've been and memories I've made.

March 2024

Kyoto, Japan

Explored ancient temples and traditional gardens. The Fushimi Inari shrine at sunrise was magical. Learned about tea ceremonies and tried my hand at calligraphy.

December 2023

Barcelona, Spain

Wandered through Gaudi's architectural masterpieces. The Sagrada Familia was even more impressive in person. Enjoyed tapas and learned about Catalan culture.

August 2023

Banff, Canada

Hiked through the Canadian Rockies. The turquoise lakes and snow-capped peaks were breathtaking. Spent evenings stargazing and learning about local wildlife.

May 2023

Bali, Indonesia

Experienced the perfect blend of culture and nature. Learned to surf in Canggu, explored Ubud's rice terraces, and participated in a traditional ceremony.
